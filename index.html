a bit about me

Hi! I'm Laurel. I'm a developer who recently transplanted to Tucson, Arizona from a small town in Kansas. I consider myself fortunate to be making a living doing what I love - building cool stuff and teaching others how to build cool stuff. Not many people get to find a career doing what they would otherwise do for fun, so I count my blessings. My interests include all things web, Android dev, UI design, and the .NET world. I also love teaching and mentoring and spend my free time volunteering at programming clubs to help others get into the best career in the world.
